First Aid & Response

Keep a first-aid kit accessible. For burns and eye incidents, follow recognised first-aid guidance and seek professional help when necessary.

Emergency Plan

  • Post emergency contacts and location clearly.
  • Ensure a clear exit route.
  • Isolate fuel/electric power if safe to do so.
