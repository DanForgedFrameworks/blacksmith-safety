Beginner Projects

Pick projects that build core skills: drawing, tapering, bending, upsetting, scrolling.

Wall Hook (taper + bend)

  1. Cut stock (e.g., 8–10 mm mild steel).
  2. Forge a clean taper; round the end.
  3. Create the hook bend over the horn; plan for fixing hole.
  4. Punch/drill fixing hole (cool workpiece first). Dress and finish.

Leaf Keyring (veining + texture)

  1. Draw out a short taper for the stem.
  2. Spread leaf with peen; add veins with a chisel/liner.
  3. Drill/punch for ring when cold; finish safely.
