Core PPE

  • Eye protection suitable for hot work.
  • Hearing protection where noise levels warrant it.
  • Protective footwear; natural-fibre clothing; avoid synthetics near heat.
  • Appropriate gloves for handling hot/warm stock (use with care at the anvil).
