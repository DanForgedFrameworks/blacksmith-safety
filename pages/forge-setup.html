Principles

  • Clear walkways and safe working distances around anvil, forge, and vice.
  • Good housekeeping: keep trip hazards and flammables away from the hearth.
  • Plan hot metal routes (forge → anvil → quench) with no crossing paths.

Layout

Triangle layout: forge – anvil – vice within a few steps; quench near the anvil but not blocking exit routes.

Checklist (printable)
  • Stable anvil and stand; secure vice.
  • Non-combustible surfaces near forge.
  • Clearance above for hammer swings.
  • Fire extinguisher & fire blanket accessible.
