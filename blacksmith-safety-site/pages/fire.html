Fire & Fuel

  • Store fuel safely and away from ignition sources.
  • Keep suitable extinguishers to hand; know how to use them.
  • Handle ash and clinker in metal containers; confirm cold before disposal.

Hot Work Awareness

Be mindful of heat soak and delayed ignition. After shutdown, continue to monitor the work area and manage embers.
