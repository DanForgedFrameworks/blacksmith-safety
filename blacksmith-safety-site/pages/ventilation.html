Basics

Provide adequate ventilation to manage heat, smoke, and fumes. Be aware of carbon monoxide (CO) risks when using solid fuel or gas forges in enclosed spaces.

Good Practices

  • Cross-ventilation or mechanical extraction where appropriate.
  • Keep fuel and burners maintained per manufacturer instructions.
  • Consider CO monitoring in enclosed spaces.
